Help Center

Welcome to the Eventify Help Center! Here you'll find answers to frequently asked questions and guidance on how to use the platform.

How do I purchase a ticket?
Go to the event list on the home page, choose your desired event, enter the quantity (only one per transaction), and click "Purchase Ticket".
What payment methods are supported?
We currently support payments via PayFast, including options like EFT, card payments, and more.
Where can I see my purchased tickets?
Navigate to the "My Tickets" section in the sidebar to view all your purchased tickets and event details.
I didn’t receive a confirmation email. What should I do?
Please check your spam/junk folder. If it’s not there, contact us using the details below.
How do I report a problem with an event?
Use the contact details below to reach our support team, and we’ll assist you promptly.
